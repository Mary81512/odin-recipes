Frosting

All recipes

Make your baking goods even more special with a delicous vanilla or chocolate frosting!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla (fresh or extract)
  • 1/2 cup soft butter
  • 2 tbs milk
  • Optional
    • chocolate
    • food colouring
    • different aromas (strawberry,lemon,...)

Steps

  1. Stir the butter with a whisk until its smooth
  2. Gradually add the sugar
  3. Add milk and vanilla
  4. If you want to add chocolate you have to melt it first and then stir it into the mixture
  5. Beat for 3-4 mins

Your frosting should be used immediatly or stored in a container at room temp for 2-3 days!
