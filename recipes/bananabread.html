Bananabread

All recipes

If you have leftover bananas you can easily make a delicious bananabread with my recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 brown bananas (mashed)
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 cup of soft butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Mix the wet ingredients
    • Milk
    • Bananas (mashed)
    • soft Butter
    • 2 Eggs
  2. Add the dry ingredients
    • Flour
    • Baking soda
    • Sugar
    • Cinnamon
  3. Mix everything until you have a smooth batter
  4. Fill it into a rectangular pan and put it in die oven for 40mins/ 180Â°C
